Patient simulators for real-world training disparities

RIB designs advanced medical training manikins and wearable simulation technology that help healthcare educators deliver realistic, hands-on learning experiences for critical clinical skills.

About us

We believe medical simulation should prepare students for the full spectrum of patients they'll encounter in practice. So we work from real 3D scan data to build patient profiles that reflect who clinicians actually treat, bringing underrepresented populations into the training environment with realism and care.

Services we offer

Realistic Clinical Scenarios

Train learners in realistic, repeatable scenarios that simulate real patient conditions, helping teams build confidence before they enter clinical environments.

Flexible Training Solutions

From portable wearable simulators to full-body manikins, our products support a wide range of training environments including simulation labs, classrooms, and field exercises.

Built for Educators

Designed with healthcare educators, our solutions align with clinical competencies and make it easier to teach complex procedures and emergency response skills.
